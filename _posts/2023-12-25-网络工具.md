### 网络测试

![Crepe](/assets/img/企业微信截图_16977812896091.png)

```shell
# 需要注意和本机不能在同一个网段下
wget https://iperf.fr/download/fedora/iperf3-3.1.3-1.fc24.x86_64.rpm
rpm -ivh iperf3-3.1.3-1.fc24.x86_64.rpm
# 服务端
iperf3  -s 
# 客户端
iperf3 -c 192.168.100.21 -B 192.168.100.22 
```
